This document describes questions that can be used to test the templated CQ approach.

Please note that *F at the beginning of a question, before a question's quotation marks, denotes an immediate follow-up asked to the agent.

A.  "Where in the city are there empty parcels of land?",
B.  "Who owns parcel 489967?",
C.  "What is the perimeter of parcel 512976?",
D.  "Where is parcel 490700?",
      *F"Generate a google maps satellite view link for this polygon"
E.  "What is the area of parcel 394769?",
F.  "What bylaws apply to parcel 389914?",
        *F"And what zoning laws apply to it?"
G.  "What buildings occupy parcel 395234 and when were they built?"
        *F"What are these buildings used for?"
H.  "How many residential buildings occupy parcel 396890?"
        *F"What is the area and perimeter of this parcel"?
